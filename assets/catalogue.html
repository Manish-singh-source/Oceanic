Marine Paint

Oceanic Premium Antifouling Paint

High-performance antifouling coating engineered to protect vessel hulls against marine growth, salt attack and prolonged water exposure.

Recommended OnBoat hulls, fishing vessels, workboats and underwater marine surfaces.
ApplicationApply over suitable marine primer on clean, dry and prepared substrate by brush, roller or spray.
BenefitHelps reduce barnacle and algae adhesion while improving coating endurance in service.
Marine Paint

Fibre Coat Plus

Reinforced antifouling system with fibre-enhanced structure for improved film integrity and better long-term marine protection.

Recommended OnFRP boats, fibre boats and marine surfaces needing stronger coating film support.
ApplicationUse after proper sanding and priming; maintain uniform film build for durable underwater protection.
BenefitImproves crack resistance and helps maintain coating consistency through maintenance cycles.
Marine Paint

Oceanic Sea Shield Antifouling

Marine antifouling protection designed for harsh salinity and bio-fouling conditions with reliable coating endurance.

Recommended OnCommercial craft, utility vessels and salt-heavy coastal operating environments.
ApplicationApply on properly sealed and primed hull surfaces with recommended recoat interval and thickness.
BenefitSupports cleaner hull performance with dependable fouling resistance in marine service.
Application Notes

Marine System Guidance

For best output, keep surface free from salt, grease, old loose paint and moisture before every coating stage.

Surface PrepPressure wash, de-scale, remove loose matter and ensure stable substrate before primer coat.
System BuildPrimer coat, intermediate support if required, then antifouling top system as per service condition.
Use AreaHull bottoms, underwater body areas and high-salinity marine operating zones.

Marine Application Process

Step 1Clean and de-rust the hull thoroughly.
Step 2Apply compatible marine primer system.
Step 3Build required coat thickness evenly.
Step 4Allow curing before launch or immersion.

Typical Use Areas

Fishing boats
Cargo vessels
Underwater hulls
Coastal craft

Functional Industrial Protection

Industrial Coating

Thermochromic + Antimicrobial QD Enamel

Smart industrial enamel combining heat-responsive visual behavior with antimicrobial functionality for technical surface applications.

Recommended OnEquipment housing, utility panels, machinery covers and technical metal surfaces.
ApplicationApply on clean primed metal using thin, even coats for quick-dry film and functional finish.
BenefitSupports visible thermal response with a cleaner, hygiene-focused surface film.
Industrial Coating

Self Polishing + Heat Reflective Epoxy

Epoxy coating system with self-polishing behavior and heat reflective performance to support durability and thermal load reduction.

Recommended OnPlant structures, exposed metal zones, industrial roofs and temperature-sensitive surfaces.
ApplicationUse over prepared substrate with recommended primer and maintain proper curing between coats.
BenefitDelivers durable epoxy film while helping reduce thermal load on exposed structures.

Industrial Application Process

Step 1Degrease and prepare the metal or technical surface.
Step 2Apply suitable anti-corrosive or epoxy primer.
Step 3Apply coating in controlled DFT and coverage.
Step 4Allow cure before handling or service exposure.
